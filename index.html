Welcome to Trillion Servers

Future of Gaming!

Trillion Servers is a Gaming Community Founded on June 10th 2019 and has been growing and expanding since then.

The Owner Griffindor and Co-Owner Mr.Money have been striving to keep Trillion Servers Growing and thriving.

We offer a variety of different game servers including servers for Unturned, Rust, and Garry’s Mod.

We also offer different kinds of servers for unturned.

Our Servers we have...

Unturned:

SemiRp - A SemiRp is for players that are looking for a little bit of roleplay and pvp.

FullRp - A FullRp is for those players wanting a Full Roleplay Experience(In Development)

Survival - A Survival is for those that love to pvp and build up bases.

Modded Survival(5x) - A Survival for those who love to pvp and find loot easily.(In Development)

Rust:

2x Loot/Gather-Our 2x rust server is heavily modded with plugins that players find helpful and a fun experience to add to rust.

Garry’s Mod:

DarkRp:This is for players that want a roleplay experience in Garry’s Mod.

Trillion Servers is hoping to expand to more servers for each game and maybe even expanding to more games to get a better variety for our Gaming Community.
